#### Where you had trouble

In practice, attempting to use a general schema for music data is extremly difficult, since the definition of simmilar data accross many platforms is borderline ambiguous, and writing the algorithm that can decifer if two tracks from two platforms are the same would take a really long time, concidering the existence of track remasters, versions, remixes, etc. can make this task difficult enough within the same platform. For this project, the current best decisicion is to comprimise and make the model more rigid.
